About QA Cinemas

Welcome to QA Cinemas, your gateway to a world of cinematic excellence. With over two decades of unwavering dedication to the magic of the silver screen, QA Cinemas stands as an industry-recognized cinema chain, pioneering entertainment since our inception in 2004.

Our Story

Our journey began with a vision to create a cinematic experience that transcends time. For the past 20 years, we have meticulously crafted a legacy of delivering the best in film entertainment. From our humble beginnings in the Northwest of England, we have grown to become a beloved destination for movie enthusiasts of all ages.

A Celebration of Film

At QA Cinemas, we take pride in curating a diverse selection of films, bridging generations and genres. We specialize in showcasing the latest blockbusters, ensuring you're among the first to witness the magic of new releases. But that's not all; we cherish the timeless classics that have left an indelible mark on the history of cinema. From the swinging '60s to the neon-soaked '90s, we bring these iconic films back to life for you to enjoy on the big screen once again.

Our Succes in the Northwest

Our success story is rooted in the hearts of the communities we serve. The Northwest of England has embraced us as their cinematic home, and we are grateful for the loyalty and love we've received. It is this support that has propelled us to consider new horizons.

A National Vision

As we set our sights on the future, QA Cinemas is poised for national expansion. But before we embark on this exciting journey, we recognize the importance of enhancing our online presence. We aim to ensure that our brand is not just known but celebrated across the digital landscape. Your online experience with QA Cinemas should be as extraordinary as your visit to our theaters.

Join Us on this Journey

We invite you to join us on this thrilling adventure. As we grow, your support and enthusiasm are the pillars of our success. Stay tuned as we unveil a reimagined online platform that will bring the magic of QA Cinemas to your fingertips.

At QA Cinemas, we are not just about watching movies; we're about creating memories, celebrating storytelling, and fostering a sense of community. Thank you for being part of our cinematic family. Together, we'll continue to make every frame count.
